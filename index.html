About me

I've always been into what happens behind the scenes. The root of things. How something starts from nothing and ends up actually working. That's the part I want to understand, and that's the kind of work I want to do.

"For me, failure is when I don't try hard enough."

Something I actually believe

I love partnerships. The actual work of them. Two companies sitting at a table, figuring out their markets overlap in ways they hadn't really thought about, and finding a deal that makes sense for both. That moment is fun to me, and I think the people who do it well don't get enough credit.

The other thing I keep coming back to is context. With AI everywhere now, pulling a number isn't really the hard part anymore. The harder part is sitting with the data long enough to actually understand the story behind it. Why did it move? Who made that call? What did the room look like when the decision got made? That's where the real work is.

And honestly, I love hearing from people who see things differently. The best feedback I've gotten on my analyses has come from people I didn't know yet, who caught something I missed and took the time to tell me. If you read something on this site and want to push back, please do. That's kind of the whole point.
