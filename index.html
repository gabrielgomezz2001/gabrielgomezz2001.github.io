Selected work

Two projects, two different problems.

I picked sports for these because the data is public and the stories are loud. That makes them a great place to test what's actually true. The same approach works on any business: question the headline, isolate the variable, find what's really moving the numbers.

No. 01 · Heat 2025/26
Operations · Lineups · Performance

Lineup analysis for the Miami Heat.

An interactive site breaking down a full season. Efficiency by position, lineup splits, court zones, and concrete recommendations for next season's rotation.

The finding
Wiggins is meaningfully better at the 4 (+5.4 net rating vs +1.8). The Bam and Ware double big posted +6.7, but the headline number was inflated by weak opponents. Real recommendations follow.

No. 02 · Beyond Miami
Sponsorships · Commercial · Decomposition

Did Messi actually lift MLS?

An interactive analysis of post Messi sponsorship growth, plus a contrarian finding the press has missed. Built so you can stress test the assumption yourself.

The finding
Inter Miami captured 26% of all new MLS sponsorship dollars by themselves. The other 28 teams grew at the rate they were already growing. The "halo" is essentially zero.
